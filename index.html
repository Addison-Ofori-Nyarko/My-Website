About Me

I am a mathematics educator and researcher from Ghana with experience teaching at primary, junior high school, and senior high school levels. I am currently teaching at Asanteman Senior High School. My work focuses on conceptual understanding, student values in mathematics education, and creating supportive learning environments that promote confidence, critical thinking, and wellbeing.

I hold a Master of Philosophy in Mathematics Education and have a strong interest in mathematics teaching, curriculum, student wellbeing, and research-informed instructional practices.
